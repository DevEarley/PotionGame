Curses & Cures

Rules

Basics

- Players start with 1 card of each type. - You must follow the rules on each card. - Players talk to each other and try to get themselves cured. - Players guess each other's cards' descriptions. 1 point per card.

Getting Points

- When a player is cured, their cards are now up for grabs and are worth 1 point each. - Only when the player is cured, each player has a chance for guessing. - The player that triggered the cure gets to guess first. - When the player guesses correctly, they hold the discarded card as a point. (It's effect is not in play.) - The game is over once everyone is cured or bored.
