Curses & Cure

Rules

Basics

- Players start with 1 card of each type. - You must follow the rules on each card.

Geting Points

- When a player is cured, their cards are now up for grabs and are worth 1 point each. - Only when the player is cured, each player has a chance for guessing. - The player that triggered the cure gets to guess first. - When the player guesses correctly, they hold the discarded card as a point. (It's effect is not in play.) - The game is over once everyone is cured or bored.

Using a D20 to play

- Players take turns rolling a D20. - If they roll lower than 15, they may make a statement or ask a question to try to get other players to cure them. Then they pass the dice. - If they roll 15 or more, they can say or ask up to 3 things. Then they pass the dice.

Using a timer.

- Set the timer for 60 seconds. - Any player can speak or ask a question during this time. - If a player is cured, reset the timer for 60 seconds. - If no players were cured after the timer finises, set the timer for another 60 seconds. - During this time, all players are cured and players try to guess.
